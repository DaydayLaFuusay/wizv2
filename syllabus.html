INTRODUCTION

This training module will provide an overview of phishing prevention, its importance in the aerospace and defense industry, and the role of IT and developers in implementing best practices. We will also touch upon the use of technology tools like Gmail, Forcepoint, and Asana in phishing prevention.

PARTS

In this section, we will delve into the concepts of phishing prevention. We will discuss what phishing is, how it can impact organizations, and the importance of preventing it.

This section will cover the best practices in phishing prevention. We will discuss various strategies and techniques that IT and developers can use to prevent phishing attacks.

In the final section, we will discuss remediation strategies for phishing prevention. We will explore how to respond to a phishing attack and how to recover from it.

CONCLUSION

By the end of this training, participants will have a comprehensive understanding of phishing prevention, including the best practices and remediation strategies. They will be equipped with the knowledge to protect their organizations from phishing attacks and to respond effectively if an attack occurs.
